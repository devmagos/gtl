Welcome To GTL Trustees

At GTL Trustees, our mission is to create, protect and preserve wealth,
leaving a legacy for posterity.

Who We Are

GTL Trustees was incorporated on the sixth of November in 1991 under the name of Afribank Trustees and Securities Limited, a subsidiary of the then Afribank Nigeria PLC.

Later the name was changed to Afribank Trustees & Asset Management Company LTD, and the company commenced operations in the year 2000.

Our Services

GTL Trustees offers a full range of directed and discretionary trustee services
and expertise for individuals as well as the corporate sector.

  • Private Trust
  • Public Trust
  • Corporate Trust
  • Wealth Management
  • Agency Services

Private Trust

We offer private trust services to our clients with a focus on professional wealth management, wealth preservation and investor services – assets are acquired or eliminated in accordance with the clients’ wishes. Services include: will trusteeship/executorship, administration of estates, living trust, child education trust, and general estate planning.

Public Trust

We act as trustees to states raising capital through bonds. Apart from managing the sinking funds to ensure coupon/principal payments to bond investors, we also provide other oversight functions of ensuring compliance to the trust deed by issuers.

Corporate Trust

  • Debenture/Syndicated Lending
  • Collective Investment Scheme(CIS)

We act as trustees to companies seeking financing from multiple banks or the public. We draw up and perfect the necessary security documentation and provide other oversight functions of ensuring compliance to trust deeds and default handling. We also act as trustees to collective investment schemes by ensuring that fund managers comply with the trust deed and other relevant regulations.

Wealth Management

We manage funds for our clients with a focus on wealth preservation and maximization in line with their respective risk appetites.

Agency Services

As the escrow agency, we act on behalf of two or more parties involved in a business transaction to hold escrow assets until certain conditions are met or obligations are satisfied.
